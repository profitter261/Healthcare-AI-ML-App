{% elif diagnosis_result.disease == 'Pneumonia' %}

{% elif diagnosis_result.disease == 'Sepsis' %}

Sepsis is a life-threatening response to infection that can cause tissue damage and organ failure.

Precautions: Immediate medical attention is critical. Maintain hygiene, and monitor any infection symptoms closely.

{% elif diagnosis_result.disease == 'Stroke' %}

Stroke occurs when blood supply to the brain is interrupted or reduced, depriving brain tissue of oxygen and nutrients.
